Melissa Perry, Soprano

About the artist:

Melissa Perry is a soprano based in New York City. She earned her BA in Music Performance from Seattle Pacific University, simultaneously completing a BA in Interior Design. Growing up as a dancer in San Diego, she was introduced to classical music at an early age; however, it was not until her freshman year at of college that she became interested in studying music. During her undergraduate years she was a two time 1st place winner in the Puget Sound NATS student adjudication and the vocal finalist in her school's 2016/2017 concerto competition. Roles Melissa has performed include “Queen of the Night” from Mozart’s Die Zauberflӧte, “Olympia” from Offenbach’s Les Contes d’Hoffman, and "Mademoiselle Silberklang” from Mozart’s Der Schauspieldirektor. She currently serves as a soloist at Trinity LES Lutheran Parish in New York City, performing oratorio and cantata selections with chamber orchestra.
